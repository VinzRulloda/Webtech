Welcome to Filipino Recipes

Filipino recipes at your disposal

Filipino cuisine is composed of cuisines of more than a hundred distinct ethnolinguistic groups found throughout the Philippine archipelago. Filipino dish draws inspiration from several influences wherein you can taste the Spanish, Chinese, India, and western influences with each dish. Filipino dishes are often described as a balance between three primary flavors: salty, sweet, and sour.
